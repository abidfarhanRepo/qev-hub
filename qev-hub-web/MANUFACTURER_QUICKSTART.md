# Manufacturer Portal Quick Start Guide

## Getting Started as a Manufacturer

### 1. Sign Up

1. Visit the QEV Hub landing page at `/`
2. Scroll to the footer section
3. Click **"Join as Manufacturer"** button
4. Fill out the manufacturer signup form:
   - Company details (name, location, contact info)
   - Business information
   - Upload verification documents
5. Submit the form
6. Your account status will be **"Pending Verification"**

### 2. Login

1. Go to `/manufacturer/login`
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

> **Note**: Customer accounts cannot access the manufacturer portal. You must complete manufacturer signup first.

### 3. Dashboard Overview

Upon login, you'll see:
- **Statistics Cards**: Total vehicles, active listings, orders
- **Verification Status**: Your current verification status
- **Quick Actions**: Buttons to add vehicles and manage orders
- **Recent Vehicles**: Your last 5 vehicle listings

### 4. Adding Your First Vehicle

1. Click **"Add New Vehicle"** from dashboard or navigate to Vehicles → New
2. Fill out the form:

   **Basic Information**:
   - Make (e.g., BYD, Tesla)
   - Model (e.g., Seal, Model 3)
   - Year
   - Vehicle Type (EV/PHEV/FCEV)
   - Price in QAR
   - Availability status

   **Technical Specifications**:
   - Battery capacity (kWh)
   - Range (km)
   - Charging time
   - Top speed (km/h)
   - 0-100 km/h acceleration
   - Seating capacity
   - Cargo space (liters)

   **Warranty & Origin**:
   - Warranty years
   - Warranty kilometers
   - Origin country

   **Description**:
   - Detailed vehicle description

3. Click **"Add Vehicle"**
4. You'll see a success message and be redirected to your vehicles list

### 5. Managing Vehicles

**View All Vehicles**:
- Navigate to Vehicles in the menu
- Use the search bar to filter by make, model, or year
- View vehicle cards with all details

**Edit a Vehicle**:
- Click the **"Edit"** button on any vehicle card
- Update the details
- Save changes

**Delete a Vehicle**:
- Click the **"Delete"** button on any vehicle card
- Confirm the deletion
- Vehicle will be permanently removed

### 6. Viewing Orders

1. Click **"Orders"** in the navigation menu
2. View all orders for your vehicles:
   - Order tracking ID
   - Vehicle details
   - Order total amount
   - Delivery status
   - Order date

### 7. Navigation

The manufacturer portal has a persistent navigation bar:
- **Dashboard**: Overview and statistics
- **Vehicles**: Manage your vehicle listings
- **Orders**: View customer orders
- **Profile**: Shows your company name and email
- **Logout**: Sign out of the portal

## Important Notes

### Verification Status

Your manufacturer account starts as **"Pending"**. While pending:
- ✅ You can add vehicles
- ✅ You can manage your listings
- ❌ Vehicles won't be visible to customers
- ❌ You won't receive orders

Once verified:
- ✅ Vehicles appear in the marketplace
- ✅ Customers can place orders
- ✅ Full access to all features

### Best Practices

1. **Complete Your Profile**: Fill in all company details during signup
2. **Quality Images**: Upload high-quality vehicle images (when feature is available)
3. **Accurate Specifications**: Provide precise technical details
4. **Competitive Pricing**: Set direct manufacturer prices
5. **Detailed Descriptions**: Write comprehensive vehicle descriptions
6. **Keep Stock Updated**: Mark vehicles as sold-out when unavailable
7. **Monitor Orders**: Check orders regularly for new purchases

### Pricing Strategy

QEV Hub supports price transparency:
- **Manufacturer Direct Price**: Your factory-direct price
- **Broker Market Price**: Typical broker/dealer price
- System will calculate savings for customers

### Vehicle Types

- **EV (Battery Electric)**: Fully electric, no combustion engine
- **PHEV (Plug-in Hybrid)**: Electric + combustion engine
- **FCEV (Fuel Cell)**: Hydrogen fuel cell electric

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Login?
- Ensure you completed manufacturer signup
- Check email/password are correct
- Customer accounts cannot access manufacturer portal
- Contact support if issues persist

### Vehicles Not Showing?
- Check your verification status
- Vehicles only visible to customers after verification
- Ensure availability is set to "Available"

### Orders Not Appearing?
- Orders only appear after verification
- Check order status filters
- Refresh the page

### Form Validation Errors?
- Make (required)
- Model (required)
- Price (required, must be > 0)
- Year (must be 2020-2030)
- Seating (must be 2-9)
